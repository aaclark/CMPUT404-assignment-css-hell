About

My name is Alain Clark, and I am not a seasoned web developer. I'm a Computing Science student at the University of Alberta, and though I spend most of my days doing schoolwork, I also spend my evenings doing schoolwork. There isn't much else I can say about that except that during the occasional lull in academic responsibility, I enjoy listening to music-- I'm a bit of an audio nut-- playing guitar, drinking excessive amounts of coffee, drawing, writing fiction, and contemplating the great meaninglessness of existence.
